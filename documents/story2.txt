Title: The Rise of Queen Seraphina

The kingdom of Arenthia is known for its rich history and powerful rulers.
Queen Seraphina, the current monarch, is renowned for her wisdom and strength.
She ascended to the throne after the mysterious disappearance of her father, King Aric.
Seraphina is determined to uncover the truth behind her father's vanishing and believes that the lost treasure of Eldoria holds the key.
She seeks the guidance of Elara, the wizard of the enchanted forest, to help her on this quest.
